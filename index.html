🍎 Toddler Quran Zone (Age 3+)

✨ Big colorful Arabic letters – tap to hear the sound! ✨

📢 For parents & teachers:
• Point to each letter and say its name out loud first.
• Let your child tap the big card – it will play a clear human-like voice.
• Repeat 2–3 letters per day. Use praise and clapping.
• Later, ask: “Where is Ba?” – let them find and tap.
🧸 Recommended: After mastering letters, move to short surahs below.
